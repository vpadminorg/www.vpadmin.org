VpAdmin: Pricing and Plans ​

VpAdmin offers a range of plans designed to meet various needs, from basic website creation to advanced management features. Choose the plan that best fits your requirements and start building your dynamic website today.

Available Plans ​

VpAdmin provides three distinct plans:

  • Free: Experience the core functionalities of VpAdmin at no cost. Create a website quickly with zero coding required. This is ideal for users who wish to deploy a simple static site and experience the convenience of one-click publishing with integrated admin features.
  • Plan A ($1/Month or $10/Year): Step up your website management with increased storage, build time, and enhanced admin capabilities. This plan is perfect for users who need to expand their site and add more management and user interaction features.
  • Plan B ($2/Month or $20/Year): Unlock the full potential of VpAdmin with more resources and full admin capabilities. This plan provides you with the most comprehensive features and is designed for users who require advanced customization, monetization, and detailed analytics.
  • Theme: See the Purchasing Integrated Private or Shared Themes section for details.

Payment Information ​

You can purchase upgrades for any number of months for your site that you need.

NOTE

The VpAdmin system defaults to Plan A. If you are purchasing Plan B or a theme integration service, please input "PlanB" or "Theme" in the message field.

Purchasing Integrated Private or Shared Themes ​

If you would like to use your own custom website theme or integrate a different public theme with VpAdmin, you can purchase the theme integration service.

  • Public Theme Integration: ($10/theme) Your theme will be added to VpAdmin and made available for use by all VpAdmin users. You will also receive a $10 gift card that can be used to upgrade any of the websites you create.
  • Private Theme Integration: ($20/theme) Your theme will be added to VpAdmin and made available for your use only when creating new websites. You will also receive a $10 gift card that can be used to upgrade any of the websites you create.

Warning

Please ensure that you hold the appropriate copyright or have purchased the necessary rights for the theme. VpAdmin reserves the right to refuse integration and provide a refund if the theme’s copyright permissions do not meet the required standards.

To purchase a theme integration service, please navigate to Settings -> General.

After your purchase is complete, you will be contacted by the VpAdmin Support Team. The email you receive will detail how to submit your template files. We will then proceed with the theme integration within 3-7 business days, and your account will be credited with a gift card.
